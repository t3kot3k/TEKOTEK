## About

**TeKoTek** is a remote-first African tech startup specializing in AI-powered solutions. We operate across four countries, publish in top-tier IEEE conferences, and partner with Japanese enterprises — all while staying rooted in the communities we serve.

**Our mission:** Empower individuals and businesses to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape through practical, accessible technology.

**Our vision:** A world where AI seamlessly integrates into daily life.
